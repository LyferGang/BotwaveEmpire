## Customization Guide

### For Each Client

1. **Update business name** in all scripts:
   - Replace "Jimenez Plumbing" with their business name
   - Update folder structure for their industry

2. **Industry-specific folders**:
   - Plumbers: Active Jobs, Invoices, Estimates, Photos
   - Electricians: Service Calls, Permits, Inspections
   - HVAC: Maintenance Contracts, Installations, Repairs
   - General: Customize based on their workflow

3. **AI Agent customization**:
   - Add their pricing to the database
   - Set up their service areas
   - Configure business hours
   - Add their contact info
